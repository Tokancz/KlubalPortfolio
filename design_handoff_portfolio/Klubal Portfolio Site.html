About me

CZ · OPEN FOR WORK
// CZ — MATYÁŠ KLUBAL

Student 3D artist,
building hard-surface worlds.

I'm Matyáš Klubal — a student, no degree yet, just a lot of renders. I model hard-surface sci-fi and like building cyber-leaning scenes: machines, vehicles, and the worlds they live in.

Most of my work lives in Blender, where I've spent the last four years learning modeling, lighting and texturing one project at a time. I'm picking up Affinity for texture and post, and just started exploring Houdini for procedural detail.
